FAQ

How do I book a ride?▼
Enter your pickup and drop location on the home screen, select your vehicle type, and tap "Find a Driver". A nearby driver will be assigned to you.
How does wallet payment work?▼
Add money to your IDapp wallet via Razorpay (UPI, cards, netbanking). Ride fares are automatically deducted from your wallet balance.
How do I become a driver?▼
Go to Profile and switch to Driver Mode. Fill in your vehicle details and upload your documents. Once verified, you can start accepting rides.
What is the cancellation policy?▼
You can cancel free within 1 minute of booking. After that, a small cancellation fee may apply depending on your vehicle type.
How does the SOS feature work?▼
Tap the red SOS button during a ride. It will share your live location with your emergency contacts and alert our safety team immediately.
I left an item in the car▼
Go to My Rides, tap the ride, and use "Report Issue". We'll help you connect with the driver to retrieve your belongings.
