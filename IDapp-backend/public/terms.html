4. Booking & Cancellation Policy

Booking

When you book a ride, you confirm that you intend to travel. Booking confirmation is immediate upon acceptance by a driver.

Cancellation

Free Cancellation Window: You may cancel without charge within 30 seconds of booking confirmation.

After the 30-second free window, cancellation will incur a ₹100 cancellation fee unless:

  • The driver cancels the ride
  • The driver has not started the ride within the allocated time

Driver No-Show

If a driver does not arrive within 5 minutes of the pickup time, you may cancel without charge. Contact support at support@idapp.in for disputes regarding cancellation charges.
